Lamb Chops with green pepper sauce

May 15, 2023

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 01.

    Start by seasoning both sides of the lamb chops with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat, then add a splash of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the lamb chops in the pan. Sear the chops for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until they develop a golden-brown crust. Adjust the cooking time based on your desired level of doneness. Remove the lamb chops from the pan and set them aside on a plate. Cover them loosely with aluminum foil to keep warm.

  • 02.

    In the same pan used for searing the lamb, add a bit more olive oil if needed. Add the minced garlic and chopped shallot. Sauté them for 1-2 minutes until they become fragrant and translucent. Pour in the beef or lamb broth and heavy cream, then add the crushed black peppercorns. Stir to combine. Allow the sauce to simmer and reduce for about 5-7 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Continue to stir occasionally. Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove it from the heat, and whisk in the butter until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy and glossy. Adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if necessary

  • 03.

    Plate the Lamb Chops Place the seared lamb chops on serving plates. Pour the creamy pepper sauce over the lamb chops, making sure to cover them evenly.

  • 04.

    If desired, garnish the dish with fresh herbs, such as thyme or rosemary, for a pop of color and added flavor.

  • 05.

    Serve the lamb chops with pepper sauce immediately while they are still warm. This dish pairs well with a variety of side dishes, such as mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh green salad.
